HTML Basics: Level 4

Create a Mission Log Page

  • Add a <form> titled “Mission Log” under a <section> tag
  • Inside the form, add a <label> and <textarea> so astronauts can write about their space mission
  • Add a <button> that says “Submit Log”
  • Use a <strong> tag to emphasize an important part of your mission summary
  • Use a <span> tag to change the color of one word in a paragraph (like making “danger” red)
  • (Optional Bonus) Customize your mini mission page to any way you see fit! Here's some ideas:
    • 1. A picture (../../photos/mission.jpg)
    • 2. A footer that says “Log last updated: [your date here]”

HTML Cheat Sheet

  • <section>...</section> – Defines a section of content
  • <header>...</header> – Intro or heading area of a section
  • <footer>...</footer> – Footer area with notes or dates
  • <div>...</div> – Groups content together
  • <hr> – Horizontal line for separating sections
  • <br> – Line break inside a paragraph
  • <ul><li>...</li></ul> – Unordered list with items
  • <h1> to <h6> – Headings (h1 is largest)
  • <form>...</form> – Creates a form to collect input
  • <label>...</label> – Describes an input field (used inside a form)
  • <textarea>...</textarea> – Multi-line text box for input (used inside a form)
  • <button>...</button> – Clickable button (often used to submit a form)
  • <strong>...</strong> – Makes text bold and important
  • <span style="color:red;">...</span> – Colors part of a sentence (inline)
