Visual Effects/Animation: Level 4

  • 📝 Hide the ".subtitle" text by setting "opacity: 0" and "transform: translateY(3rem)"
  • 💡 Hide the "h1" text using "h1::before" by setting its "background" to "var(--bg-color)"
  • 💡 Create the typing animation on "h1::before" using typewriter (Cheat Sheet for syntax)
  • ✨ Add a caret to "h1::after" by setting a small "width" (like "0.125rem") and a "black" background
  • ✨ Animate the caret in "h1::after" by using both "typewriter" and "blink" animations
  • 📝 Add the "fadeInUp" animation to ".subtitle" and delay it using:
    "animation-delay: calc(var(--typewriterSpeed) + 2s)"

Visual Effects/Animation Cheat Sheet – Level 4

  • 📝 Subtitle Styling
  • "animation: fadeInUp 2s ease calc(var(--typewriterSpeed) + 2s) forwards;" – Animates the subtitle to fade in and move upward after typing finishes
  • 💡 Typewriter Cover Line
  • "animation: typewriter var(--typewriterSpeed) steps(var(--typewriterCharacters)) 1s forwards;" – Animates the text reveal in "h1::before"
  • "::before" – Used to cover and reveal "h1" text as the typewriter effect progresses
  • "background: var(--bg-color);" – Makes "::before" the same color as the background to hide the text
  • ✨ Caret Line
  • "::after" – Used to create a blinking caret at the end of the animated text
  • "width: 0.125em;" – Makes the caret line thin and realistic
  • "background: black;" – Makes the caret visible in "::after"
  • "animation: typewriter var(--typewriterSpeed) steps(var(--typewriterCharacters)) 1s forwards, blink 750ms steps(var(--typewriterCharacters)) infinite;" – Animates the caret in "h1::after" to follow the typing and blink continuously
